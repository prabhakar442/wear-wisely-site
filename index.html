Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

How does the service work?

We offer a seamless 4-step process: book your service through our app or website, we pick up your laundry at your preferred time, professionally clean your items, and deliver them back to your doorstep fresh and ready to wear.

How many days does it take to process?

Standard laundry service takes 24-48 hours. Dry cleaning times vary by garment type but typically take 2-3 days. We offer express services with 24-hour turnaround for urgent needs.

Do you offer free pickup and delivery?

Yes! All our services include free doorstep pickup and delivery during our standard operating hours. We provide specific time windows for your convenience.

Can you deliver in 24 hours?

Absolutely. Our Express Laundry service guarantees 24-hour delivery for an additional fee. This service is available for orders placed before 12 PM for next-day delivery.

How are clothes measured for pricing?

Laundry services are priced by weight (per kilogram) after cleaning. Dry cleaning is priced per individual garment. All items are weighed and counted in your presence during pickup.
